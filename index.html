How it Works?

When you start TapTap's extension, it starts listening with you. When you think
you want to save the best part, just press 'highlight', and TapTap will start saving 10 sec
before you pressed 'highlight'. (Magic, right?). Do this how many times you want to save only
the best parts of your meetings, podcasts, and YouTube videos.

Start the "Listening"

Press "Highlight" to save the best parts

End the "Listening" and have your highlights ready
